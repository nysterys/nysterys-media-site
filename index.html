How We Collaborate

We believe the best partnerships feel invisible. Our talent creates content that is authentic by default, and any collaboration has to fit naturally into that. We do not do hard sales pitches, scripted deliverables, or restrictive briefs. If it looks like an ad, it is not a fit.

Music & Labels

Our preferred partners. We work directly with record labels, independent artists, and bands looking for genuine fan-first promotion. If the music is good, the content writes itself.

Beauty & Cosmetics

Products our talent actually uses and loves. We partner with beauty and cosmetic brands where the fit is organic, giving audiences real recommendations they can trust.

Fashion & Clothing

Style is already part of our creators' content DNA. Clothing and fashion collaborations work when the brand matches the aesthetic, not the other way around.

Authentic Content Only

Every engagement, sponsored or otherwise, follows the same rule: if it does not feel natural in our creators' feed, we pass. Our audience's trust is not for sale.

We do not work with AI app promotions, low-quality or counterfeit brands, dropship operations, or any partner whose products do not meet a genuine quality bar. We also pass on anything that feels inauthentic, off-brand, or misaligned with our values. We keep our roster's credibility intact by being selective about who we say yes to.
